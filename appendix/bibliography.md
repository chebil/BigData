# Bibliography and Further Reading

## Books

### Big Data

1. Mayer-Schönberger, V., & Cukier, K. (2013). **Big Data: A Revolution That Will Transform How We Live, Work, and Think**. Houghton Mifflin Harcourt.

2. White, T. (2015). **Hadoop: The Definitive Guide** (4th ed.). O'Reilly Media.

3. Karau, H., et al. (2015). **Learning Spark**. O'Reilly Media.

### Machine Learning

4. Hastie, T., Tibshirani, R., & Friedman, J. (2009). **The Elements of Statistical Learning** (2nd ed.). Springer.

5. Bishop, C. M. (2006). **Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning**. Springer.

6. Murphy, K. P. (2012). **Machine Learning: A Probabilistic Perspective**. MIT Press.

7. Géron, A. (2019). **Hands-On Machine Learning with Scikit-Learn, Keras, and TensorFlow** (2nd ed.). O'Reilly Media.

### Data Science

8. McKinney, W. (2017). **Python for Data Analysis** (2nd ed.). O'Reilly Media.

9. VanderPlas, J. (2016). **Python Data Science Handbook**. O'Reilly Media.

10. Wickham, H., & Grolemund, G. (2017). **R for Data Science**. O'Reilly Media.

### Statistics

11. Wasserman, L. (2004). **All of Statistics**. Springer.

12. James, G., et al. (2013). **An Introduction to Statistical Learning**. Springer.
